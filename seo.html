First of all I'm assuming you don't know SEO. Well, I'm just saying - SEO is a system that follows your system to bring your website to the first page of Google search engine. Apart from Google search engine, there are many other search engines like Bing, Yahoo, etc.

SEO is divided into three parts:

White hat or White Hat SEO.
Gray hat SEO or gray hat SEO.
Black hat SEO or black hat SEO.

White Hat -
is a system that is compliant with Google's rules, called White Hat SEO or White Hat SEO.

Gray Hat SEO -
The system that does not comply with some of Google's guidelines and does not follow SEO is called Gray Hat SEO or Gray Hat SEO.

Black Hat SEO -
Black hat SEO or black hat SEO is the system that is practiced by SEO in compliance with Google's rules.

Fundamentally SEO can be divided back into two. In that case, the division will be:

Paid SEO
Organic SEO.

We will not discuss paid SEO. Because paid SEO is - you pay Google $ to rank your web site on the first page of Google, Google will put your site on the first page.

As much work as organic SEO. Organic SEO is again divided into two ways:

On-page SEO.
Off-page SEO.

On-Page SEO -
The work you do after your website is ready, that is, the work done to rank your website in Google, is called on-page SEO.

For example - uploading new content to your website, increasing the site's loading speed, fine-tuning the site's Url and more.

Off-page SEO -
The work done from outside your site. They are tucked away in the pages of SEO. Some of these are - getting your site linked up with another site that relates to your site, posting about your site on various social media, etc.
